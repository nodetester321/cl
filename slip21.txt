Q1. What legal actions can a content creator take if their work is illegally downloaded or streamed online in India?
Preserve evidence of piracy—screenshots, URLs, download/stream links.

Send takedown notices to websites/platforms hosting the content.

File complaint with cyber police/cyber crime authorities.

Take action under Indian Copyright Law for copyright infringement.

Seek injunction/court order to block infringing websites/content.

Claim damages/compensation from offenders where applicable.

Request platform disclosure of infringer details if legally permitted.
