Q1. Report on “Cyberbullying and Legal Action in India: A Case Study of Legal Remedies for Victims”
Introduction:
Cyberbullying means harassment, threats, humiliation, or abuse through digital platforms. Indian cyber laws provide remedies to victims.

Case Study:

A student is harassed repeatedly through fake social media accounts and abusive messages.

Personal photos are posted online without consent.

The victim collects evidence and files a complaint with cyber police.

Legal Remedies:

Complaint can be filed under the  and applicable criminal laws for harassment/defamation.

Platforms can be requested to remove abusive content/accounts.

Electronic evidence like screenshots/chats can be used in investigation.

Courts/police may take action against offenders.

Conclusion:
Indian law provides victims with legal remedies to report abuse, remove harmful content, and seek punishment of offenders.
