Q1. Report on “Cyber Defamation and Legal Repercussions: A Case Study of Social Media Misuse”
Introduction:
Cyber defamation occurs when false or harmful statements are published online to damage a person’s reputation.

Case Study:

A person creates fake posts/social media content spreading false allegations about another individual.

The victim suffers reputational and emotional harm.

The victim reports the incident to cyber police.

Legal Repercussions:

Action can be taken under the  for certain cyber offences depending on conduct.

Defamation may also be prosecuted under relevant provisions of the Indian Penal Code / Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita for reputational harm.

Electronic records/screenshots can be used as evidence.

Impact:

Criminal/civil liability for offender

Removal of defamatory content

Compensation/damages may be sought

Conclusion:
Cyber laws help protect individuals from online defamation and misuse of social media.
