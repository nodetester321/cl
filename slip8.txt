Q2. Steps students should take to ensure online activities comply with the IT Act, 2000
Understand basic cyber laws and prohibited online conduct.

Use licensed software and legal digital resources only.

Do not engage in hacking, phishing, or identity misuse.

Protect personal and institutional data from unauthorized disclosure.

Obtain permission before using copyrighted or private materials.

Avoid posting defamatory, obscene, or unlawful content online.

Maintain proper cyber hygiene and secure devices/accounts.

Report suspicious cyber incidents to authorities/institution.

Stay updated on institutional and legal compliance requirements.
