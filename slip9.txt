Q1. How can a student protect themselves legally when using educational apps/websites that collect personal information (Indian Cyber Law)?
Read privacy policies before sharing personal information.

Use only trusted/verified educational platforms.

Share minimum necessary personal data.

Avoid uploading sensitive documents unless required.

Use strong passwords and enable MFA where possible.

Do not share login credentials with others.

Report misuse of personal data to platform/institution immediately.

Keep records/screenshots of suspicious activity for evidence.

Know rights under the  regarding data privacy and misuse.

Q2. How would you create a policy for password management and data protection?
Require strong passwords with minimum length/complexity.

Mandate periodic password changes if policy requires.

Enforce Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).

Prohibit password sharing among employees.

Require secure password storage/password managers.

Define access control based on employee roles.

Mandate encryption for sensitive data.

Establish backup and recovery procedures.

Provide employee training on password/data security.

Conduct regular audits for compliance.
