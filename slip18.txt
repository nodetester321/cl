Q2. What steps should the government take immediately after a cyber attack on its infrastructure?
Isolate affected systems/networks to contain the attack.

Activate incident response and disaster recovery plans.

Inform relevant cybersecurity agencies (e.g., CERT-In / national cyber response teams).

Preserve logs and digital evidence for forensic investigation.

Assess impact and identify compromised systems/data.

Patch vulnerabilities and remove malicious access.

Restore services from secure backups.

Notify affected departments/stakeholders.

Strengthen monitoring to prevent further attacks.

Conduct post-incident review and improve defenses.
