Q1. Report on “Impact of the Indian IT Act, 2000 in Addressing Cybercrime: A Case Study of a Data Breach Incident”
Introduction:
The  provides the legal framework to address cybercrimes and data breaches in India.

Case Study:

A company suffers a data breach due to unauthorized access by hackers.

Sensitive customer information is stolen and leaked online.

The organization reports the incident to cyber authorities.

Role of IT Act, 2000:

Section 43 – Compensation for unauthorized access/data theft.

Section 66 – Punishment for hacking/computer-related offences.

Section 72A – Punishment for unlawful disclosure of personal information.

Section 43A – Company liability for failing to protect sensitive data.

Electronic evidence is admissible in court.

Impact:

Helps investigate and prosecute offenders

Encourages companies to improve cybersecurity

Provides legal remedy for victims

Conclusion:
The IT Act strengthens cybercrime enforcement and promotes data protection in organizations.
