The Information Technology Act,
2000 addresses a company’s data breach 
by providing legal provisions for punishment 
and compensation. Under Section 43, unauthorized access or data theft leads to financial compensation. Section 66 makes such acts criminal offences like hacking. If a company fails to protect sensitive data, it is held liable under Section 43A for negligence. Section 72 punishes breach of confidentiality and privacy.

the Act ensures legal action against offenders, compensation to victims, and forces companies to adopt better security practices.
