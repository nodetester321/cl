Q2. Penalties for companies violating data protection and privacy laws in India
Compensation Liability – Companies may be ordered to compensate affected users for negligence in protecting sensitive data.

Fines/Penalties – Regulatory penalties may apply depending on the violated law/regulation.

Criminal Liability – Responsible individuals may face prosecution for unlawful disclosure/misuse in certain cases.

Civil Actions – Users may sue for damages/injunctions where applicable.

Regulatory Orders – Authorities may direct corrective actions, audits, or compliance measures.

Reputational/Business Impact – Loss of trust, contracts, and customers.
